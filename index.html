New Comprehensive Medical Clams Management

Each medical review carried out by MedixCorp consist of four main components.

Medical Eligibilty

Pertains to the review of medical policy definithions such as chronic, cosmetic or ecperimental. These definitions are constantly changing and ofter unclear.Applying them to a specific claim requires medical expertise and deep understanding of the patient's condition. Preformingn this check is an essential part of the Meidx claims review.

Medical Validity

Ensures that the proposed treatment is in face recognised as a valid option for the patient's diagnosis and condition.Our physicians asses each claim and apply to it experience, expertise and internationally recognised treatment guidelines.

Medical Necessity

Includes assesing whether or not a valid treatment is necessary given the patient's individualy situation. As with validity, internetional guidelines are applied as well as soecialist expertise and experience.

Length Of Stay Managment

Our necessity and validity review applies to specific treatments and inpatient stay alike. Through this method, we are able to strike an optimal balance, keeping the patient admitted while requierd, and discharging immediately thereafter in oder to prevent further complications.
